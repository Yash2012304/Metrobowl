Our Story

A window into
bowling's past.

"Mickey is the last of a dying breed — his knowledge and expertise are second to none in the area." — David R., Customer

Metro Bowl has been serving bowlers on Broadway in Fair Lawn since 1960. Owner Mickey has spent over 50 years perfecting the craft of ball fitting and drilling — and it shows. Whether you're picking up your first ball or you're a seasoned league player, you'll leave with exactly what you need.

Beyond balls, Metro Bowl carries a full selection of bags, shoes, accessories, and trophies. No waiting on shipping, no guessing at sizing — just real expertise, in person, today.

What We Do

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🎳
Ball Fitting & Drilling

Mickey sizes and fits your hand in minutes, then drills on the spot. Most customers are on the alley the same night. Beginner or pro — the fit is always right.

🛍️
Balls & Accessories

A comprehensive inventory of professional-grade equipment: reactive balls, entry-level options, bags, shoes, tape, inserts, and more — all in stock, ready to take home today.

🏆
Trophies & Engraving

Custom trophies, plaques, and engraving for leagues, tournaments, and awards. Excellent engraving work with fast turnaround — ask Mickey about your next event.

🔧
Ball Repair & Resurfacing

Cracked, nicked, or losing its reaction? Bring it in. Mickey's decades of experience mean your equipment gets the care it deserves.

👟
Bowling Shoes

From entry-level to performance shoes, sized and fitted in-store. Stop guessing online — try them on and walk out ready to bowl.

💬
Expert Advice

Not sure what you need? Just ask. Mickey's spent 50+ years helping everyone from first-timers to competitive bowlers find the right setup. No hard sell, ever.
